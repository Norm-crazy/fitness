I sat with my co-worker (we will call him JT), during our breaks and lunches and we would go over workout routines and dieting. My workout routine

My workouts are broken up into Push-Pull days, with a day of Cardio. Even on my Push-Pull days, I would do some Cardio and stretch before and after lifting weights. I start with low weights, and I increase the weights after each set.
