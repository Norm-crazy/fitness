My Workout

This page, I will go over my workout routine

Back to the gym

I started to get to know my co-workers at my new job, and it turns out that one of them is a Licensed Personal Trainer. I sat with my co-worker (we will call him JT), during our breaks and lunches and we would go over workout routines and dieting. I started my gym membership at Planet Fitness, and JT set me up with a modified StrongLifts 5Ã—5 workout plan, StrongLifts 5X5 are sets of five reps.

My workout routine

My workouts are broken up into Push-Pull days, with a day of Cardio. Even on my Push-Pull days, I would do some Cardio and stretch before and after lifting weights. I rotate between different workouts, and when lifting weights, I start with low weights and I increase the weights after each set,
